What's Up Myrtle?

What's Up Myrtle is a front-end app and demo for the Grand Strand API Project. Starting in 2017 the GSAP began compiling a database of all businesses, events, and attractions for the Grand Strand and beyond. This span quickly grew from the NC border out through Conway (with some in Aynor), and as far south as Georgetown.
